Statistics

Teen suicide is one of the top reasons people die in the US. Suicide happens in all ages, and all races. Almost 50% of the suicides involve a firearm. This costs the U.S. annually, 51 billion. Everytime someone suicides, there is around 25 attempts.
People over the age of 20 tend to commit suicide more frequently than those under the age of 20. You can see that during 2000-2015, age 85 and older have the highest suicide rate.
The ethnicity of people who suicide, Whites and American Indians are the older race that commit suicide.
Male youth die by suicide 4.34 times more than a female youth. In 2010, 4,600 people ages 15-24 died to suicide. Firearms and Suffocation were the most used ways to commit suicide. Many people who think about suicide and may go through with it will give away a lot of their possessions, show an obsession with death, and making comments where they sound like they won't be around soon.
